Kait Rhoads

Kait Rhoads utilizes a method of construction that considers the individual units and forms coming together to form a connected, fluid or floating object. The concepts develop slowly and the process of creating a complete piece can take months or even years. Her work is emotionally affective and offers a sense of wonder as the audience interacts.
